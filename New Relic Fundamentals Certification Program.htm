New Relic Certification

Validate your knowledge and understanding in performance monitoring with the New Relic platform and gain recognition amongst peers and employers. .

New Relic Certification validates performance monitoring and observability expertise to help individuals and organisations showcase their technical skills and knowledge with the world's first Observability Platform, and help to deliver a more perfect internet.

Get the skills and knowledge to use New Relic to see what's happening in your application, understand where the problems are, discover opportunities for optimizing your entire application ecosystem, and make sure you never miss an issue that needs attention.

New Relic Fundamentals Certification

This certification will mark you out as a New Relic Certified Practitioner and demonstrates you have the fundamental knowledge and skills with the New Relic observability platform, as well as fundamental skills and knowledge in products such as APM. There will be separate certifications available for other products coming soon like Infrastructure and Insights so you can choose your preferred product to focus on, or collect them all !

  • New Relic Fundamentals Certification - APM

  • New Relic Fundamentals Certification - Infrastructure (coming soon)

  • New Relic Fundamentals Certification - Insights (coming soon)

Earn your Certification in three steps

New Relic Fundamentals Certification Program is made up of three assessments which are taken in sequence. The first one tests knowledge across a range of observability topics and products like APM, Browser, Syntetics, Infrastructure and Insights. The second one is the largest assessment and is focused on a product (currently APM but there are other options coming soon). The third assessment tests knowledge on setting up and managing alerts in New Relic.

To help you learn and prepare, there are free online courses for each assessment below. Or use the Practise Tests in each section to test your knowledge. If you think you are ready you can start the assessment right away!

Assessment 1

Performance Monitoring with New Relic

This tests broad knowledge of the New Relic Platform and how it works – from what each product monitors and what data they collect, to the ways they work together to giving you a complete picture of your whole architecture.

Recommended prepration materials:

  • Performance Monitoring Fundamentals Online Coursework & Practise Tests
  • Instructor Led and Virtual Workshops
  • Webcasts and Learncasts

Assessment 2

New Relic Product Assessment - Select one

Choose the product for which you'd like to be certified and select the relevant assessment below. The product currently available for certification is New Relic APM. But stay tuned - we will have more certifications rolling out soon!

Recommended prepration materials:

  • New Relic APM Fundamentals Online Coursework & Practise Tests
  • Instructor Led and Virtual Workshops
  • Webcasts and Learncasts

Assessment 3

New Relic Alerting

This assessment will ensure you know how to create an alert policy to fit your monitoring needs, set critical thresholds, and set up your notification channels so issues get addressed by the right people right away.

Recommended prepration materials:

  • New Relic Alerting Fundamentals Online Coursework & Practise Tests
  • Instructor Led and Virtual Workshops
  • Webcasts and Learncasts
